ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Davit, freelancer in programming and writing. I have been writing programs of all sorts for more than a year and I have been writing essays of any kind for more than 5 years. I am quick to adapt to any situation and feel extremely passionate about learning new things all the time.

I have graduated from the university and ever since the 2nd year of my studies, I was always looking forward to getting as much information as possible. I have studied communications, writing and linguistics. I love writing essays, books, reading different articles and always improving my skills. I believe that writing is an artform which can always be improved, whether it's technical, finction or simple argumentative essay.

When I got interested in programming, I started doing lots of research and studied a lot on my own. I started programming as a hobby, which involved into something bigger as I kept on learning and improving, coding every single day, reading books and taking courses. I always felt hungry for more and more information and as I kept on learning the fundamentals of programming, I realized that this is something which I want to be able to do at a high level.
