ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Davit, a freelance programmer.

Ever since I was a child, I was always trying to learn as much as I could. During the 1st year of my studies in university I took my passion towards learning more seriously and started developing myself and getting better each day. I have now graduated and I keep on doing what I love, which is coding, learning and reading

When I got interested in programming, I started doing lots of research and studied a lot on my own. I started programming as a hobby, which involved into something bigger as I kept on learning and improving, coding every single day, reading books and taking courses. I always felt hungry for more and more information and as I kept on learning the fundamentals of programming, I realized that this is something which I want to be able to do at a high level.

My hobbies are reading, music, video games, photography, philosophy and art.
